Chicken Burrito

This chicken burrito recipe is simple and easy to prepare for a quick bite in a hurry!

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla
  • 1/4 cup of cheese
  • 1/4 cup of beans
  • 1 cup of chicken
  • 1/4 cup of rice
  • taco seasoning to taste

Preperation

  1. First coat the chicken with taco seasoning and then cook on pan at medium heat until tender.
  2. Heat up the toritlla until lightly warm.
  3. Start by placing rice onto the tortilla, followed by beans and chicken.
  4. Sprinkle cheese in the end and then roll up the burrito.
  5. Place rolled burrito back onto the pan and cook until slightly crispy and cheese has melted.
