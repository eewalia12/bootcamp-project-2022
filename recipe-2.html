Grilled Cheese

This grilled cheese recipe is perfect for someone on the go!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/2 cup of cheese
  • 1/8 cup of butter

Preperation

  1. First coat the pan with a small layer of butter.
  2. Place cheese on one slice of bread and then cover with the other slice.
  3. Spread butter over both outer sides of the sandwich.
  4. Place the sandwich on the pan and cook on medium heat. Flip once the side is cooked until golden brown.
  5. Once both sides are crispy and golden brown, remove from pan and enjoy!
