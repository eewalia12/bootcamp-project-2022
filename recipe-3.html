Apple Pie

Traditional apple pie recipe best served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 apples
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 1/8 cold water
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 egg yolk

Preperation

  1. Slowly combine flour, butter, and cold water, making sure to keep a hard consistency to the dough. Once fully combine, refridgerate for an hour.
  2. Chop up the apples into slices and mix in cinnamon, sugar, and lime juice.
  3. Remove dough from the fridge and cut in half. Roll out both halves into equal sized circles.
  4. Cover all sides of baking tray with butter and then layer one of the circles into the tray, covering the entire base.
  5. Fill the baking tray with the apple mixture and cover with the other half of dough.
  6. Use the egg yolk to coat the top of the pie with egg wash, and cut small slits into the middle of the pie.
  7. Bake pie for 45 minutes at 350 degrees or until golden brown.
  8. Remove pie from oven and let it cool for 15 minutes before serving!
